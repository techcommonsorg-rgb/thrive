Our Purpose

Supporting every Meriden student

"A safe and accessible platform where students can monitor their wellbeing, learn coping strategies, and easily access help when needed."

Adolescence is a challenging time, with pressures from academic work, friendships, and personal expectations. Thrive@Meriden provides a thoughtful, private space designed specifically for Meriden students.

Integrated into the school's pastoral care program, the app complements existing support systems — giving students tools to understand themselves better, develop healthy habits, and reach out when they need it most.

Built with care, empathy, and the Meriden community in mind.

The Proposal

A message to Mrs Brennan

Proposal for a Student Wellbeing App

Adolescence is a time when many students experience significant academic and social pressures. While Meriden already provides strong pastoral support, a dedicated wellbeing app could further enhance this by giving students easy access to resources at any time.

The app would include daily mood check-ins, self-assessment tools, guided breathing and mindfulness exercises, and clear information about accessing school counsellors and pastoral staff.

By integrating the app into the pastoral program, students would have a private way to monitor their wellbeing and develop healthy coping strategies — encouraging early help-seeking and a stronger sense of support throughout school life.

Thank you for considering this proposal.
